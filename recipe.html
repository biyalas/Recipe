Healthy benefits of oats =>
Oats are mainly a carbohydrate. 100 grams of oats provide around 389 calories of energy. Oats are rich in essential nutrients and minerals like thiamine, zinc, magnesium, iron, manganese, phosphorus and selenium. A bowl of oatmeal provides a substantial part of the body‚Äôs daily requirement of fibre, essential fatty acids, vitamin E and protein. As a great source of whole grains, oats contain a heart-protective starch known as beta-glucan that aids in reducing high cholesterol and the risk of certain cancers.
When you use flour made of oats in place of all-purpose flour it is almost a 1 to 1 ratio exchange for most recipes. For some recipes, you may want to add a bit more or a bit less to get the consistency that you are looking for. The texture is slightly different than regular flour, but the health benefits far exceed that of regular flour. You will also notice a definitive taste difference when you are using oat flour in recipes. Most of us have been conditioned to expect a particular taste or texture to food that we cook or bake with regular flour. However, it is delicious and beneficial for the human body.
